TTS system in baseball broadcast scenario

Mar 2019 - Mar 2021

NCSOFT has owned a baseball team and was very interested in baseball. In 2020, they also won the Korean Baseball League (KBL) championship. At NCSOFT, we studied "broadcasting-style" TTS system that can imitate sportcasters. To express the diverse speaking style of baseball broadcasting, especially in pitch ranges, expressive speech synthesis technique was needed. We studied TTS system that can generate speech with various emotions and its intensity. Furthermore, we also develp the TTS system to generate speech with proper prosody corresponding to text symbols (,, ~, !, ?).
