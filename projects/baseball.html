TTS system in baseball broadcast scenario

Mar 2019 - Mar 2021

I contributed to research and develop a multi-speaker TTS system that can generate various idols' voices (about 100 idols) in one TTS system. The TTS systems are used in following two services included in UNIVERSE.

1. Fan Networking Service (FNS): The K-pop idols write a post with photos and short comments (like Instagram), and the TTS system reads these comments in the idols' voices.

2. Private Call: Provides fake phones calls to the fans with their favorite idols’ voices. The TTS system was used to generate the voice of idols’ phone calls.
